Fried rice with tofu
------------------------------------------------------------
Category: Vegetarian
Suitable for Vegans: No
------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients: 
- Left over rice 100.0 gram
- egg 1.0 pc
- Corn 250.0 gram
------------------------------------------------------------
Cooking Instructions:
1. Beat and fry the eggs
2. Add the rice
3. Add the corns
4. Wok and fry everything together!
============================================================
